Minetest (Private)

This page is about Novimatrem's Minetest server.
New to Minetest? Please consult the Minetest Wiki at https://wiki.minetest.net (mirror: [backup at archive.org])
Please be sure to set a unique player skin for yourself in the Skins tab of the inventory/crafting menu.

Rules:
- Be excellent to each other.
- Have fun!
- No politics.
- Be kind to all.
- English only in chat.
- Respect everyone.
- Use common sense.
- If something isn't yours, leave it alone unless you have permission.
- Don't leave floating trees.
- Re-plant trees you chop down only for resources, where possible.
- Don't de-forest the land, unless you're going to put something in that space.
- Don't defile the land.
- If you have a creeper explode and cause block damage, you must repair it. Be it damage to the generated land, or damage to a creation- repair any creeper damage that your player entity triggers to happen.
- Repair enderman block damage if you see it (it's breaking 1 block, and placing it in the right place).
- Do not idle/go AFK in a place where creepers can get you.
- Be nice about Novi's weight

Tips:
- Holding a torch generates light without even having to place it.
- When you die, you can use /wp and choose to 'Display' "Death waypoint", to be directed to exactly where you died.
- I advise putting your house somewhat near to spawn, and staying well-armed and well-armoured at all times.
- You can use Texture Packs on servers! I recommend the 'Pixel Perfection' pack, which you can find from the in-game easy ContentDB installer menu, pick 'content', and 'Browse online content' from the main menu, and sort by texture packs, be sure to enable the one you chose in the content menu, and restart the game, before joining.
